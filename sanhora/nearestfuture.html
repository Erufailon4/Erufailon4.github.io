Nearest Future Audio from
15th Anniversary Special Site

「To let the irreversible time reach the "Near Future" Horizon, one must first go through the "Nearest Future."
To race through it like some did, to wish for it like some did, it depends on your interpretation.」

By the time I realised it, I had already started running
While my heart trembled within me
The white shadow grew distant behind me
But I didn't turn back to look even once

I gathered that the season of lives fading away
Would pierce through my heart in time
And as the deep darkness drew nearer
I felt I would never see daylight again

My heart itself felt like it was floundering
So I caught it in my arms, holding onto it desperately
And the long slope leading up towards the starry sky
I truly thought it could continue on for all eternity

The flowers' blossom and decay
Repeat on and on, ever since the age of gods
But I've not once
Found that to be beautiful

The transient shadow continues to scramble for
The spring sun, up in the sky
But I've not once
Found beauty in its efforts

I live on, all the while resigning to my fate
As if not wanting anything at all...
But I never let go
Of that final wish of mine, and continued running up...
